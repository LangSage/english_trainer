Simple order for learning

First students hear 1–20. Then they practice 10, 20, 30 ... 100. After that, mixed numbers become much easier.

Step 1 — Basic numbers
Train 1–10 and 11–20 with easy multiple choice.
Step 2 — Tens pattern
Practice ten, twenty, thirty ... one hundred.
Step 3 — Mixed numbers
Move to 21–99 and then full 1–100 typing practice.
